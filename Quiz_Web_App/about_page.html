Who is this website for?

Geography is about spatial distribution of phenomena. It is best learnt through interaction with maps. This is an attempt to provide visual medium to spatial learners. This website is targeted mainly at people who want a quick way of refreshing important concepts related to Indian and World geography. It is a learning tool for geography enthusiats, quizzers and exam aspirants.

What is included in the paid version?

The paid version includes access to all the maps as well as more content to be added in the future.

Where did the idea come from?

I am a UPSC Civil Services aspirant, appearing for mains with Geography optional. During my preparation, I felt the need for interactive India specific maps to internalise geographical locations like National Parks as well as visualise historical events like Indian National Congress sessions and battles. Also, maps are beautiful. The application was developed by my husband and me to help me with exam preparation. We have scaled up the app for use by other aspirants.
